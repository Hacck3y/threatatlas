The **Stock Analysis** panel (internal id `stock-analysis`) is the dashboard's per-ticker deep-dive: one analysis at a time, with a structured synthesis of signal (buy / hold / sell), price targets, analyst consensus, recent upgrades and downgrades, insider transactions, and a price sparkline.

## What the panel shows

- Symbol and price
- Signal badge
- Thesis bullets
- Analyst consensus
- Price targets
- Recent upgrades and downgrades
- Insider transactions
- Sparkline history

Panel id is `stock-analysis`; canonical component is `src/components/StockAnalysisPanel.ts`.

## Availability

The panel is enabled in the full and finance variants. It is not included in the tech, commodity, or happy variants.
